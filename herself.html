In terms of her own work, she explores transformations in two ways. The first one is heavily inspired by the materials she works with. She enjoys using
chance operations and found objects to create a cohesive work. These works aren’t particularly conceptual. They concentrate on celebrating traditionally
non-art materials in an artistic manner. There is an element of exploring connections here too. Storm’s always been the type of person to humanise objects,
so these material experiments are an attempt to learn about the characteristics of a material and linking its significance and abilities to her concept…
they’re a symbol of respect and understanding for the parts that make the whole… after all, it is the specific abilities of these materials that allow for
them to mimic concepts outside the realm of materiality
