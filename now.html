let me try my best to interpret this new work… At the moment she has been focusing on escapism and surrealism, with the world as it is right now. She seems to prefer
digital mediums as they have the power to overcome the limitations of being experienced at a particular time and space. She creates these works as timestamps of her
avoiding hardships, growth, worries, etc. This work is selfish in that sense, it’s her way to distract from the consequences of the space she finds herself in. The space
she occupies now was once familiar… not anymore, now it feels hostile and she wishes to depict that… her methods are definitely bizarre and use light-hearted bits to
distract from the truth… I believe this work would be more impactful if she confronted the hostility and found a way to depict her experience with familiarity and
unfamiliarity as she does otherwise
