... she has adored art her entire life… it started by going to museums with her mother… in the beginning, she’d rush through the work until
she found something pretty and then just stare at it… a little older and she’d try to maintain a slower pace with her mother making a complex,
contemplating face because that’s how you correctly view art… a couple more years and she would actively look up exhibitions, obsessing over
every painting, every didactic, taking pictures (if permitted), and writing little notes… now it was her mother who had to wait for her to be
done… oh how the tables turned
